## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Nobleco Software is a comprehensive business management platform designed to streamline operations for jewelry and gemstone businesses. The system provides two main interfaces: an **Admin Panel** for business management and a **User Portal** for sales associates and team members.

**Current Status:**
- Core functionality: **95% Complete**
- User interface: **Fully Functional**
- Mobile optimization: **In Progress**
- Advanced features: **Under Development**

The platform enables administrators to manage products, orders, users, commissions, and business operations while providing sales associates with tools to track clients, manage orders, view products, and monitor their earnings.

## COWORKER & PERMISSIONS SYSTEM GUIDE

### Understanding Coworkers

**What is a Coworker?**  
A coworker is a special type of admin account that has limited access to the admin panel. Unlike full administrators who can access everything, coworkers can only access specific sections based on permissions assigned by administrators.

**Why Use Coworkers?**  
Coworkers allow you to delegate specific responsibilities to team members without giving them full administrative access. This improves security and ensures team members only see what they need for their job.

---

### Creating a Coworker Account

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Admin Users**
   - Go to **Users** ‚Üí **Admin Users**
   - You will see two tabs: "Admins" and "Coworkers"

2. **Create New Coworker**
   - Click the **"Add Coworker"** button
   - Fill in the coworker information:
     - **Name:** Full name of the person
     - **Email:** Their email address (used for login)
     - **Phone:** Contact number (optional)
     - **Password:** Create a secure password
     - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter the password
   - Click **"Create Coworker"**

3. **Assign Permissions**
   - After creating the coworker, find them in the coworker list
   - Click the three dots (‚ãØ) menu next to their name
   - Select **"Edit Permissions"**
   - A modal will open showing all available pages

4. **Select Permissions**
   - Check the boxes next to pages the coworker should access
   - Available pages include:
     - **Dashboard:** Overview statistics
     - **Users:** Manage regular users
     - **Clients:** View and manage clients
     - **Products:** Manage products
     - **Categories:** Manage product categories
     - **Orders:** View and manage orders
     - **Commission:** View commission settings
     - **Withdraw Request:** Process withdrawal requests
     - **Discount Code:** Manage discount codes
   - Click **"Save Permissions"**

### Best Practices

1. **Principle of Least Privilege**
   - Only grant permissions that are necessary for the job
   - Start with minimal permissions and add more if needed

2. **Regular Review**
   - Periodically review coworker permissions
   - Remove access when responsibilities change
   - Deactivate accounts for employees who leave

3. **Clear Communication**
   - Inform coworkers about their access levels
   - Provide training on how to use their assigned sections

4. **Security**
   - Use strong passwords for coworker accounts
   - Never share admin passwords
   - Monitor coworker activity through the dashboard
